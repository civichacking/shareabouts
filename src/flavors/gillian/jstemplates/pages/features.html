Features

HealthAction has five location types designed to enhance your health mapping experience.


doctor doctor…

This feature allows users to find or add an location where a doctors office or clinic exists that either accepts walk-ins or is open nights and weekends.



healthy treats…

This feature allows users to find or add an location where they have found healthy treats, for example, a new food cart that sells dishes with fruits and veggies, a restaurant or food vender that gives out samples, or a church that serves a healthy meal once a month.



healthy activities…

This feature allows users to find or add an location where healthy actives are taking place, for example, a running group that meets at a particular location every saturday morning, a free yoga class in the park, or a community center that has a great after school program.



a good place for…

This feature allows users to add an location that would be a good for a new clinic, a new food cart (outside of a elementary school for example), or a saturday morning health adventure class for kids.



an interesting instance of…

This feature allows users to look at or add an location where they  were surprised to see for example, a hand sanitizer dispenser at their local bodega, or street art with a community health theme, or a health advertisements in a unsuspected location.
