Features and Benefits

 

For you, the active community member,

 

·      a simple map interface, to explore submitted locations and add your own

·      the site design works well on small screens like your phone!

 

 

For me, the gatherer of local knowledge,

 

·      flexible text and layout, making it easy to translate the user interface text or customize the sentences used

·      data contributed by users is easier to interpret because of the organization by geographic location

·      requests for assistance can be directed to appropriate NGOs and government

 

 

For the common good,

 

·      innovation for community outreach and engagement

·      accessible on personal computers, phones, at school, or the library

·      see who else is concerned about similar issues, and see what they are doing to address it

·      improve efficiency of recovery with a new avenue to communicate local knowledge
